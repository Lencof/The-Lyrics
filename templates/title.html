The Lyrics

Rules: First you'll pick a genre, then you'll pick from two random songs in that genre. Whichever song you pick will have some lyrics missing. You'll have 1 minute to compete the missing lyrics. The faster you complete the lyrics, the more points you'll earn. If after 1 minute you haven't finished the lyrics you'll be automatically sent back to the genres page to select your next genre. Once you've chosen a song from all genres, if your score is 50,000 or higher, you'll be given a bonus song. Some say it's the greatest song of all time!! Good Luck and enjoy!
